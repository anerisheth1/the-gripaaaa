# Meet the AAAA Team

### Andrea
![Andea]({{"/assets/andrea.jpeg" | relative_url}}){: width="150px" style="float: left;
margin-right: 20px; border-radius: 50%;"}
Andrea is an EECS student interested in using robotics in the context of medical and assistive technologies. 

Andrea designed and built the custom gripper from scratch. She assembled the mechanical components and programmed the arm controls on an Arduino for 4 DOF movement allowing the pick, place, and rotate motions of the gripper.
